Highlighted Software Applications:

Pharmaxy App

I am proud to present Pharmaxy, a pharmaceutical application that I built for my client using Kotlin for Android. This app is designed to make maintaining, searching for, and selling Pharma products as smooth as possible. In addition to its user-friendly interface, Pharmaxy leverages the capabilities of the Firebase Machine Learning Kit to enable easy product billing and the ability to search for drugs by scanning their sachets. The security of my client is a top priority, which is why Pharmaxy includes OTP verification for added protection. With Pharmaxy, my client can streamline their pharmaceutical management process and improve their overall efficiency. It was an honor to be part of the team that developed this innovative solution.

Pro-Driver Application

Pro Driver is a custom android app built for ProtoEnergy, a leading provider of LPG gas. This stable and scalable app, developed with Kotlin and the MVVM design pattern, is used by drivers to deliver gas to warehouses and containers across the country. Pro Driver is not just a delivery app - it's part of a comprehensive supply chain management platform that has supported ProtoEnergy's team of over 2000 employees, including factory workers, distribution fleet personnel, and sales staff, in serving more than 50,000 retail merchants. With Pro Driver, ProtoEnergy's delivery process has been streamlined and made more efficient. I am proud to have developed this solution.

Pro-Inventory Application

Pro Inventory, a cutting-edge android app designed to be used by warehouse coordinators to streamline essential tasks such as stock taking, scanning QR codes on gas Cylinders, clocking into geofence, receiving, and loading LPG gas to trucks that replenish containers serving over 200 regions. With built-in payment integrations, Pro Inventory is a seamless, end-to-end solution that is part of a comprehensive supply chain management platform. This platform has already supported ProtoEnergy's team of over 2000 employees, including factory workers, distribution fleet personnel, and sales staff, in serving more than 50,000 retail merchants.
